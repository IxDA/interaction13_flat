Social Innovation with Impact

» Watch the Interaction13 talks online «

Interaction13 brought together almost 900 people from 28 countries for IxDA’s 6th annual conference. The four-day program explored how interaction designers impact the social fabric of our communities, organizations, and society. Watch the presentations online via IxDA’s Vimeo channel.

» Interaction13 Redux «

Redux events bring the Interaction talks and ideas back to IxDA local groups so that the inspiration and knowledge from the conference can reach as many people as possible. These events are organized through the individual initiative of local leaders in their communities. Take a look at the participating cities for 2013.
