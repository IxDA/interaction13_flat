IxD13 Planning Committee

Introducing the Interaction13 planning committee! The past 18 months have brought together this team of design leaders who volunteered their time and energy to plan Interaction13. In addition to full time employment (the jobs that pay the bills), the Interaction13 Planning Committee Leads took on the task to lead committees, each focusing on different aspects of planning IxD13. Lots of time, energy and passion has gone into planning this event for our global IxDA community.
